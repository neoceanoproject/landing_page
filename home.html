NeOceano Priorities

  1. Safety — protection from rogue waves, hurricanes & the like.
  2. Water — drinkable seawater.
  3. Seafood — sustainably sourced seafood.
  4. Power — clean, renewable ocean energy.
  5. Shelter — homes that will stand the test of time.
  6. Land — organic, floating earth.
  7. Landfood — organic, sustainable farming above the sea.
  8. Governance — work with the Ethereum Institute to ensure safe, sane governance.
  9. Remediation — of the sea and air
